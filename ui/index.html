Glossary

what the numbers mean
GT
Ground Truth — the human-labeled text + polygon from the labelme JSON sidecar. This is the reference, not what OCR produced.
PR / Predicted
What the OCR engine actually returned: polygon, text, and a confidence score.
TP / FP / FN
Detection outcomes after matching predicted lines to GT lines by polygon overlap (IoU ≥ 0.5). TP = predicted line that overlaps a GT line. FP = predicted line with no matching GT (extra / spurious). FN = GT line the engine missed. TP↑ FP↓ FN↓
Detection F1
Harmonic mean of detection precision (TP / (TP+FP)) and recall (TP / (TP+FN)). 1.0 = every line found with no extras; 0.0 = nothing found or everything wrong. ↑ better · 1.0 perfect
CER (matched)
Character Error Rate on matched line pairs. Edit distance between predicted and GT characters, divided by GT length. 0.0 = identical, 1.0 = every character wrong. ↓ better · 0.0 perfect
WER (matched)
Word Error Rate on matched line pairs. Same idea as CER but at the word level. ↓ better · 0.0 perfect
Joined-page CER
Coarse CER computed by joining all GT lines into one string and all predicted lines into another, then comparing. No alignment — order matters. Useful as a smoke check; noisier than per-line CER. ↓ better · 0.0 perfect
Mean conf
Average OCR confidence score on matched lines. High conf + high CER means the engine is confidently wrong. ↑ better · 1.0 max
IoU
Intersection-over-Union of two polygons, via their axis-aligned bounding boxes. ≥ 0.5 counts as a match. ↑ better · 1.0 identical
Missed
In the drill-down: a GT line the OCR did not detect. Drawn dashed red. fewer ↓ better
Spurious
In the drill-down: a predicted line with no matching GT. Drawn dashed amber. fewer ↓ better
Matched
In the drill-down: a predicted line that overlaps a GT line. Drawn indigo. more ↑ better
